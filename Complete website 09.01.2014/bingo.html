The Sea View Hotel's Bingo Baby Event!


The Sea View Hotel has a wonderful bingo event that can happen 4 nights a week, excluding monday. Guests of any age attend this event in hope of winning, and in search of a fun and livly activity!

The Bingo is usually best suited for guests over the age of 7 because younger children may not understand how to play and cause distruption.


Bingo is played using cards or books:


  • 1 card
  • 5 cards
  • Book (10 cards)
